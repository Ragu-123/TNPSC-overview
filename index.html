About TNPSC

The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC) is a constitutional body responsible for recruiting personnel for various state government positions in Tamil Nadu through competitive examinations. Established in 1929 as the Madras Service Commission, it became TNPSC in 1970, making it India's first Provincial Public Service Commission. It operates under Articles 315 to 323 of the Constitution of India.

TNPSC's functions include advising the government on recruitment rules, appointments, promotions, transfers, and disciplinary matters. It also conducts departmental exams and oversees certain national defense-related admission exams.

Categorization of TNPSC Exam Groups

TNPSC exams are categorized into Groups (1, 2, 2A, 3, 3A, 4) which generally reflect the seniority, responsibility, and pay scales of the positions. This structure helps candidates align their aspirations and preparation strategies.

TNPSC Group 1: High-Ranking Administrative Services

This section provides detailed information about TNPSC Group 1 examinations, which are conducted to recruit candidates for the highest administrative and leadership positions within the Tamil Nadu state government. These roles involve significant policy-making and implementation responsibilities.

Group 1 posts include prestigious roles such as:

  • Deputy Collector
  • Deputy Superintendent of Police (Category 1)
  • Assistant Commissioner (Commercial Taxes)
  • Assistant Director of Rural Development

These roles span general administration, law enforcement, revenue, rural development, and specialized technical fields.

TNPSC Group 2 & 2A: Combined Civil Services Examination-II

This section covers TNPSC Group 2 and Group 2A posts, which include a mix of administrative and subordinate roles. A key recent change is the discontinuation of interviews for Group 2 posts and different Mains exam formats for Group 2 (descriptive) and Group 2A (MCQ).

Important Update:

  • Interviews for Group 2 posts have been discontinued. Selection is based on Mains marks only.
  • Group 2 Mains: Descriptive Type.
  • Group 2A Mains: MCQ Format.

Job Types and Roles

Group 2 Posts (Examples - previously interview posts):

  • Junior Employment Officer, Probation Officer, Assistant Inspector of Labour, Sub Registrar Grade-II, Special Assistants (Vigilance & Anti-Corruption).

Group 2A Posts (Examples - non-interview posts):

  • Municipal Commissioner Grade-II, Assistant Section Officer (Secretariat, Law, Finance, TNPSC), Senior Inspector of Co-Operative Societies, Audit Inspector, Revenue Assistant, Personal Clerk, Steno-Typist.

Eligibility Criteria

üë§Age Limit (General): Typically 21 - 30 years (varies for certain posts like Sub-Registrar Grade-II: 20-30 years; Probation Officer: 22-40 years).

üë•Age Relaxation: No upper age limit for ST/SC/SCA/Destitute Widows/MBC/BC/BCM for many posts. Differently-abled: 10 years relaxation. Veterans: up to 48 years.

üéìEducational Qualification: Graduation degree in any discipline from a recognized university. Some posts may have specific degree requirements (e.g., Law for certain Assistant Section Officer roles).

Exam Process

Two stages: Preliminary Exam and Main Written Exam.

Preliminary Exam (Common for Group 2 & 2A):

  • Type: Objective (200 questions, 300 marks, 3 hours)
  • Sections: General Studies (75 Qs, Degree Std), Language (General Tamil/English - 100 Qs, SSLC Std), Aptitude & Mental Ability (25 Qs, SSLC Std).
  • Min. Qualifying Marks: 90 for all categories.

Main Written Exam:

For Group 2 (Descriptive Type):

  • Paper 1: Tamil Eligibility Test (SSLC Std, 100 marks, 3 hours, qualifying).
  • Paper 2: General Studies (Degree Std, 300 marks, 3 hours, marks considered for selection).

For Group 2A (MCQ Format):

  • Paper 1: Tamil Eligibility Test (SSLC Std, 100 marks, qualifying).
  • Paper 2: General Studies (Degree Std, 150 marks), General Intelligence & Reasoning (SSLC Std, 60 marks), Language (General Tamil/English - SSLC Std, 90 marks). Total 200 MCQs, 1.5 marks each.

Detailed Syllabus

General Studies (Degree Standard):

  • General Science, Current Events, Geography of India, History & Culture of India, Indian Polity, Indian Economy, Indian National Movement, History, Culture, Heritage & Socio-Political Movements in Tamil Nadu, Development Administration in Tamil Nadu.

Language (General Tamil or General English - SSLC Standard)

Aptitude and Mental Ability (SSLC Standard)

Paper 1: Tamil Eligibility Test (SSLC Standard)

Paper 2: General Studies (Degree Standard):

  • Modern History of India (ref: Tamil Nadu), Tamil Society ‚Äì Culture & Heritage, Social Issues (ref: Tamil Nadu), Science & Technology in Development, Constitution, Polity & Governance (ref: Tamil Nadu), Geography of India, Environment, Biodiversity & Disaster Management (ref: Tamil Nadu), Indian Economy (ref: Tamil Nadu).

Paper 1: Tamil Eligibility Test (SSLC Standard)

Paper 2:

  • General Studies (Degree Standard)
  • General Intelligence and Reasoning (SSLC Standard)
  • Language (General Tamil or General English - SSLC Standard)

Salary Structure and Allowances

Salary range typically INR 37,200 to INR 117,600 (varies by post level, e.g., Level 8 to Level 19). Includes basic pay, DA, HRA, TA, Medical Allowance, Pension, etc.

TNPSC Group 3 & 3A: Combined Civil Services Examination-III

This section details Group 3 and 3A examinations, which recruit for specialized roles often requiring specific departmental knowledge or skills. These are generally single-stage written examinations.

Job Types and Roles

  • Station Fire Officer
  • Assistant Supervisor of Industrial Co-operative Societies
  • Store Keeper (Employment and Training Dept. / Industries and Commerce Dept.)
  • Junior Inspector of Co-operative Societies

Responsibilities include tasks like managing cooperative societies, storekeeping, and fire station operations.

Eligibility Criteria

üë§Age Limit: Min 18 years. Max for General: typically 32 years (e.g., for Jr. Inspector). Station Fire Officer: 20-30 years for all categories (no relaxation).

üë•Age Relaxation: For Jr. Inspector: up to 35 years for SC/ST/MBC/BC/BCM/DW. For Asst. Supervisor & Store Keeper: No max age limit for categories other than general.

üéìEducational Qualification: Varies.

  • Store-Keeper (Industries & Commerce): Pre-University or Higher Secondary.
  • Jr. Inspector of Co-op Societies: Intermediate/Pre-University OR Commerce Degree OR Higher Diploma in Co-op Management. (SSLC for reserved categories with preference for higher qualifications).

üìèPhysical Fitness: Certificate required, specific vision standards for some posts.

üìúLinguistic Qualification: Adequate knowledge of Tamil.

No prior work experience generally required.

Exam Process

Single-stage written examination. No negative marking. Total 300 marks, 3 hours.

  • Part A: Tamil Eligibility cum Scoring Test / General English (SSLC Level - 100 Qs, 150 marks).
  • Part B: General Studies (75 Qs) + Aptitude & Mental Ability Test (SSLC Level - 25 Qs). Part B total 150 marks.

Detailed Syllabus

General Studies (Higher Secondary Standard):

  • General Science (Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology)
  • Current Events (National, International, TN specific)
  • Geography (India, Universe)
  • History and Culture of India and Tamil Nadu
  • Indian Polity
  • Indian Economy (and TN trends)
  • Indian National Movement

Aptitude & Mental Ability Tests (SSLC Standard)

Tamil Language / General English (SSLC Level)

  • Tamil: Social Issues, Socio-Political movements in TN, Tamil Society, TN Geography, Human Development in TN, Role of TN in freedom struggle, E-governance in TN.
  • English: Figures of Speech, Poetry, Grammar, Vocabulary, Prose, Biographies.

Salary Structure and Allowances

Typically Pay Level 10: Rs. 20,600 to Rs. 75,900. Allowances include DA, HRA, Medical, Pension, TA, CCA.

TNPSC Group 4: Entry-Level Posts

Group 4 examinations are the gateway to various entry-level administrative, clerical, and field positions in the Tamil Nadu government. These are highly sought-after roles due to their accessibility.

Job Types and Roles

  • Junior Assistant (Security/Non-Security)
  • Village Administrative Officer (VAO)
  • Typist, Steno-typist (Grade-III)
  • Bill Collector, Junior Revenue Inspector
  • Forest Guard, Forest Watcher (and variations)
  • Personal Clerk, Assistant, Field Assistant, Junior Executive (Office)

These roles are spread across numerous departments like Ministerial Service, Judicial Service, Secretariat, Forest Department, etc.

Eligibility Criteria

üéìEducational Qualification: Pass in SSLC (Matriculation).

üë§Age Limit (General): 18 - 30 years (VAO: 21-30 years). Specific age limits for Forest posts.

üë•Age Relaxation: MBC/DC/BC: up to 32 years. SC/ST/DW: up to 35 years. No max age limit for certain categories for posts other than VAO, Forest Guard etc. if they possess higher than SSLC qualification.

üìúTechnical Qualification: For Typist/Steno-typist: Pass in Government Technical Examination in Typewriting/Shorthand.

üìèPhysical Requirements: Specific standards and endurance tests for Forest Guard/Watcher posts.

‚úìCitizenship & Language: Indian citizen, proficient in Tamil.

Exam Process

Primarily a single-stage written examination, followed by document verification. Physical tests for Forest posts. No negative marking.

Written Exam:

  • Mode: Offline, OMR based, Objective type.
  • Duration: 3 hours. Total Marks: 300 (200 questions).
  • Min. Qualifying Marks: 90 for all categories.
  • Pattern:
    • Part A: Tamil Eligibility-cum-Scoring Test (SSLC Std - 100 Qs, 150 marks).
    • Part B: General Studies (SSLC Std - 75 Qs) + Aptitude & Mental Ability Test (SSLC Std - 25 Qs). Part B total 150 marks.

Subsequent stages: Document Verification, Physical Certificate/Skill Verification, and Physical Standards Verification/Endurance Test (for specific posts).

Detailed Syllabus

Tamil Eligibility-cum-Scoring Test (SSLC Standard):

  • Grammar, Literature, Comprehension, Vocabulary.

General Studies (SSLC Standard):

  • General Science, Current Events, Geography (India & TN), History & Culture of India, Indian Polity, Indian Economy & Development Admin in TN, Indian National Movement, History, Culture, Heritage & Socio-Political Movements of Tamil Nadu.

Aptitude & Mental Ability Test (SSLC Standard):

  • Simplification, Percentage, HCF, LCM, Ratio & Proportion, Simple/Compound Interest, Area, Volume, Time & Work, Logical Reasoning, Puzzles, Dice, Visual Reasoning, Alpha-Numeric Reasoning, Number Series.
